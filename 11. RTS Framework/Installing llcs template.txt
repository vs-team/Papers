
    Download the Zipped files from Springer's site.
    Extract the files anywhere on your computer.
    Select the files splncs.bst, splncs_srt.bst, and splncs03.bst and cut them.
    Make a folder named splncs under C:\Program Files\MiKTeX 2.9\bibtex\bst.
    Paste the three files from Step 3 into the folder C:\Program Files\MiKTeX 2.9\bibtex\bst\splncs.
    Now cut the remaining extracted files from Step 2.
    Go to C:\Program Files\MiKTeX 2.9\tex\latex and create a new folder named llncs.
    Paste the files from Step 6 into C:\Program Files\MiKTeX 2.9\tex\latex\llncs.
    Go to Start -> All Programs -> MiKTeX 2.9 -> Maintenance(Admin) -> Settings(Admin).
    Under the General tab click on Refresh FNDB.
